About Campus-2

A dedicated campus for professional and self-financing courses.

Campus-2 is situated at NH-91 Delhi Bulandshahr Road, Dariyapur, Bulandshahr. It has a three-storey building, excellent infrastructure and high-tech facilities. The campus functions under the parent society “Ishwar Dayal Parsandi Devi Vidhya Prasarni Sabha”.
